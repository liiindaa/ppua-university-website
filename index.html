Cambodia's leading accredited arts university

From classical Khmer dance to digital design, PPUA carries a thousand years of Khmer creativity into the future — with internationally recognised accreditation and partnerships from Seoul to São Paulo.

Choose a university built around your art.

At PPUA you'll find a dynamic environment where students are empowered to explore their artistic passions, drive technological advancements, and lead with vision and creativity. Join us and transform your potential into reality.

Real-world creative practice

Studios, stages and labs where you learn by making — exhibitions, recitals and showcases every semester, open to the public.

Heart of Phnom Penh

Based in Boeung Keng Kang 1 on Street 51 — minutes from the galleries, venues and creative companies of the capital.

Five faculties, one community

Arts & Design, Dance & Theater, Education, Information Technology, and Music — connected by a shared creative culture.

Events & performances

Recitals, showcases and open sessions run all year. Come and see the university at work.
